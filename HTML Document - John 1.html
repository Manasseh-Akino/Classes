Movies everyone should watch

The Chronicles of Narnia is a very cool movie to watch. You should check it out. It's best if you see it in the cinema.
One of my favorite movies in the world is Flushed Away. It's an animation that talks about the problem with materialism,
and the importance of family. It's not about how much you have but who can share it with.


Another wonderful movie you can watch is Over the Hedge. It's a very funny animation that delves into the subject
of treating people well. It's a great family movie to watch and enjoy together. Be ready to have lots of laughs!
